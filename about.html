The story of brand

In the 1970s, the newly minted brand "storms" Paris. The first branded ready-to-wear shop started operating when Roberto was not even thirty years old. He was noticed and loved for originality: the style of the brand could not be confused with anything else. Wildlife scenes, amazing sets, intricate designs on plain denim, precious metals used in clothing. The new brand did not leave indifferent such "fashion giants" as Hermes and Pierre Cardin.

1972: The first Cavalli boutique opens in Saint-Tropez, France. Along with women's collections, the company produces men's and children's clothing, as well as interior and household items.

Competent advertising has become an important point in brand promotion. The "face" of the company was the inimitable Brigitte Bordeaux.

In 1980, the designer acquired a chief assistant and companion in his business; Roberto's wife, Eva DÃ¼hringer, became him.

1998 was a turning point for the brand. It was then that a new distribution line appeared. Within the main brand, new brands have been created, such as RC (menswear), Angels and Devils (clothing for children), Class (jerseys), Freedom (outdoor clothing). The assortment is replenished with new lines of footwear, swimwear, underwear, glasses, watches, interior items, perfumes.

In 2002, the Cavalli brand was awarded the title of "Best Men's Brand", and Cavalli himself became the Designer of the Year.

Recently, the owners of the brand decided to turn to the horse theme in the work of the brand, because the founder of the brand was a great lover of these animals. So a collection of bags appeared on the market, in which each model was named after a famous breed. These are bags like Celine Orlov, Celine Merens and Celine Lipizzan. These stylish accessories have become the dream of all European fashionistas.
